Application Process

  1. Schedule a visit
    • Parents interested in Washington University Nursery School should contact the school to schedule a visit.
    • 314-935-6689
    • nursery@wustl.edu
  2. Fill out the application the application fee
    • You may fill out an Application for Admission online. You may also print out and return the pdf version: Application for Admission WUNS
    • An application fee of $40 is required when applying to the nursery school. After this is received, your child is placed on an enrollment list.
  3. Begin the enrollment process
